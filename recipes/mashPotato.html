How to Make Mash Potato From Scratch

Description

Creamy, fluffy mashed potatoes are the ultimate comfort food and side dish. Made with buttery potatoes whipped to perfection, they pair well with any main course. A hint of garlic or herbs can elevate the flavor. Perfect for weeknight dinners or festive feasts.

Ingredients

Mashed Potatoes

  • 2 pounds Yukon Gold or Russet potatoes
  • ½ cup whole milk (warm)
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Optional Add-ins

  • 2 cloves garlic (minced or roasted)
  • ¼ cup sour cream or cream cheese
  • Fresh chives or parsley for garnish

Steps

Mashed Potatoes

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into chunks. Place them in a large pot and cover with cold, salted water.
  2. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium and cook for 15–20 minutes, or until fork-tender.
  3. Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot.
  4. Add butter and warm milk. Mash until smooth and creamy using a potato masher or hand mixer.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Mix in any optional add-ins if using. Serve hot, garnished with herbs if desired.
